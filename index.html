My name is Omar Syed, and I am a junior studying Computer Science at the University of Central Florida. Over the past year, I have successfully led the design and development of several cross-platform apps, focusing on delivering high-quality user experiences. I thrive in fast-paced, team-oriented environments, and I enjoy taking on challenges that push me to learn and grow as a developer.

My work led to a 15% increase in new clients and a 95% retention rate for the company. Additionally, I have experience working in a legal consultancy role where I developed a Windows desktop app that streamlined the consultation process, significantly reducing call times and improving overall efficiency.
